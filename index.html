New Homes in Florida

Florida has fantastic weather, with sunshine year round. With over 663 miles of beaches, Florida residents are never more than 60 miles from the ocean. On top of the beautiful beaches, Florida has over 1,000 golf courses, making it hard to stay bored. If you think you can handle all the recreation available, and want to make Florida your home, NewHomeSource can help.

Real Estate Markets in Florida

Orlando has the largest selection of new homes to choose from with over 300 new home communities, perfect for anyone who wants to live by the beach and some of the world’s most popular theme parks. Near the border of South Carolina is Jacksonville-St. Augustine, a metropolitan area with beautiful views and a thriving economy. A little farther inland is theLakeland-Winter Have Area. The Sarasota-Bradenton Area has over 200 new home communities for you to explore, and the Tampa-St. Petersburg Area is an international metro area with almost 400 neighborhoods.
